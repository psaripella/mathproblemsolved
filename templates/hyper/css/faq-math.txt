<h2>Frequently Asked Questions:</h2>

Math Problem Solved will never get your financial information - so there is never any need or opportunity to store that information.&nbsp;</span>

<p style="margin: 0in 0in 7.5pt 0.25in; line-height: 15pt;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">No. These companies guard their accounts like the crown jewels. <p style="margin: 0in 0in 7.5pt 0.25in; line-height: 15pt;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">No. See crown jewels above.</span>
</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 7.5pt; line-height: 15pt;"><i><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">4. What is the difference between Guest, Registered User, Customer?</span></i>
</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 7.5pt 0.25in; line-height: 15pt;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">Guest will get a small sample of the problems &amp; solutions that Math Problem Solved provides.</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 7.5pt 0.25in; line-height: 15pt;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">Registered will have access to a greater sample of the&nbsp;problems &amp; solutions that Math Problem Solved provides.</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 7.5pt 0.25in; line-height: 15pt;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">Customer will have access to all the&nbsp;problems &amp; solutions that Math Problem Solved provides. In addition, customers will have the ability to send problems and have them solved and put on the website (sorry - no private tutoring sessions).</span>
</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 7.5pt; line-height: 15pt;"><i><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">5. &nbsp;Why are the answers/hints not provided on a single page:</span></i>
</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 7.5pt 0.25in; line-height: 15pt;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">The purpose of Math Problem Solved is to enable &amp; encourage students to make an attempt to solve the problem, rather than just have it all in one place. The multiple steps allows for the student to follow along without just getting the answer.</span>
</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 7.5pt; line-height: 15pt;"><i><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">6. How many problems does the Math Problem Solved have?</span></i>
</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 7.5pt 0.25in; line-height: 15pt;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">Too many to count - and growing.</span>
</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 7.5pt; line-height: 15pt;"><i><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">7. &nbsp;How does this compare against similar learning websites?</span></i>
</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 7.5pt 0.25in; line-height: 15pt;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: 'Helvetica','sans-serif';">The goal of all the learning websites is the same - to explain material in a manner that aids understanding and retention.
